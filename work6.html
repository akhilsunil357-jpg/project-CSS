IMPORTANT NOTICE

The school management is pleased to announce that the Annual Day Celebration of our school will be held in the month of March. This much-awaited event is one of the most important occasions in the academic calendar and is celebrated every year with great enthusiasm, joy, and active participation from students, teachers, and parents. The Annual Day provides an excellent platform for students to showcase their talents, creativity, and confidence through various cultural and academic activities. It is a day that reflects the overall growth and achievements of the students and highlights the collective efforts of the entire school community.

The Annual Day program will include a wide range of activities such as classical and western dance performances, group songs, instrumental music, drama, skits, mime shows, and other cultural presentations. These performances are designed to encourage students to express themselves artistically while also helping them develop teamwork, discipline, and stage confidence. Students from different classes will be given equal opportunities to participate, ensuring inclusiveness and broad representation. In addition to cultural programs, the event will also feature a prize distribution ceremony to recognize students who have excelled in academics, sports, arts, and other extracurricular activities throughout the year.
